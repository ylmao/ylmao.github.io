Yunlong Mao

Assistant Professor in Computer Science
Department of Computer Science
Nanjing University
Nanjing, 210023, China

I am a research member of COSEC, led by Prof. Sheng Zhong. Before that, I was a visiting scholar in College of William & Mary, working with Prof. Qun Li. I got my Ph.D. and B.S. degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University in 2018 and 2013 respectively. My research interests include but not limited to, computer networks, machine learning, security and privacy.
